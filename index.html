Babes In Brunchland

Babes In Brunchland is a fun and stylish PWA that allows users to search for a 'brunch-spot' by location and pricepoint. The user can then browse through a quick list of all the matching results. Next they can click to get details, including a map, and link to menu. Once they have found the best spot they can create a custom invitation to download and share.

This was a collaborative group project.
